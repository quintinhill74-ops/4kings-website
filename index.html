FAQ

Quick answers. Less back-and-forth. More cleaning.

Do I need to be home?

Nope. Many clients provide access instructions. If you prefer to be home, that’s fine too.

Do you bring supplies?

Usually yes. If you want us to use your products (allergies, preferences), tell us in the notes.

How do you price jobs?

Square footage + condition + scope (deep clean vs standard) + add-ons. Clear, no surprises.

What areas do you serve?

Edit your service area in the footer + schema tag. You can list cities, counties, or a radius.

What if I’m not satisfied?

Tell us quickly. We’re fix-it-focused—quality control is part of the process.

Do you do same-day?

When availability allows, yes. Submit a request and mark it urgent in the notes.

Ready for a clean you can feel?

Let’s align on scope, timeline, and expectations—then execute. That’s the whole playbook.
